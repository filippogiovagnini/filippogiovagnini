##  Hi, Im Filippo

Im a PhD candidate in **Stochastic Analysis** at **Imperial College London** and a part-time **ML Engineer** at @AletaIndex.

My work sits at the boundary between **rigorous probability theory** and **practical, data-driven methods**. I focus on numerical methods for stochastic PDEs, interacting particle systems, and mean-field limits, with applications ranging from fluid dynamics to machine learningdriven financial models.

Alongside academia, I work on applied **NLP and representation learning for financial markets**, building scalable pipelines that turn unstructured text into predictive and explainable signals.

---

##  Research Interests
- Stochastic PDEs and particle approximations  
- Interacting particle systems and mean-field limits  
- Numerical methods for stochastic dynamics
